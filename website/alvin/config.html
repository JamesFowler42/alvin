Name Toggle

The menu names are used on your Pebble screen. They are also translated to the IFTTT Maker 'event name'. They are made lowercase and spaces become underscores. For example, menu 'My Bedroom Light' becomes event 'my_bedroom_light'.

If 'toggle' is ticked, then the menu item will have a checkbox on your Pebble screen. Turning off will send an '_off' suffix, turning on will send an '_on' suffix. For example, 'my_bedroom_light_off' and 'my_bedroom_light_on' respectively.

Clicking 'save' will fire an IFTTT Maker event called 'alvin_test', to allow you to confirm the key is correct.
